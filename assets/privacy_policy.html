Privacy Policy

LetterLoo - ABC Fun Learning

Introduction

Welcome to LetterLoo. We are committed to protecting your child's privacy and your right to privacy. LetterLoo is an interactive educational application designed to help children aged 3-6 learn the alphabet through engaging games and activities. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application. Please read this privacy policy carefully.

Information We Collect

LetterLoo is designed with privacy in mind and does NOT collect personal information:

  • NO Personal Information: We do not collect any personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, photos, or location data.
  • NO User Accounts: The app does not require user registration or login.
  • Local Data Only: All game progress, scores, and settings are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to external servers.
  • NO Tracking: We do not track user behavior, usage patterns, or analytics.
  • NO Third-Party Access: No data is shared with third parties.

How We Use Your Information

Since LetterLoo does not collect any personal information or data, there is no information to use, process, or share. The app operates entirely offline and all data remains on your device.

App Features

LetterLoo includes the following educational activities:

  • Letter Tracing: Practice writing letters with guided tracing
  • Matching Game: Match letters with corresponding objects and animals
  • Sorting Challenge: Arrange letters in alphabetical order
  • ABC Storybook: Learn letters through interactive stories with emojis and descriptions

All progress in these games is stored locally on your device.

Your Child's Privacy

LetterLoo does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from children or adults. All app data stays on your device. There is nothing to access, update, or delete from our servers because we don't have any.

Data Storage

All data generated while using LetterLoo is stored locally on your device:

  • Game progress and scores
  • Completed activities and levels
  • User preferences (sound settings)

This data remains exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to external servers or shared with any third parties.

Third-Party Services

LetterLoo does NOT:

  • Use third-party analytics services
  • Display advertisements
  • Integrate with social media platforms
  • Connect to external servers
  • Share data with any third parties
  • Include in-app purchases or payments

The app is completely self-contained and operates independently without any external connections.

Data Security

All app data is stored securely on your device using standard device security measures. Since no data is transmitted or stored on external servers, there is no risk of data breaches from our application. We recommend:

  • Keeping your device's operating system updated
  • Using device lock features (PIN, password, biometric)
  • Being cautious with device permissions

Data Retention and Deletion

To delete all app data:

  • Simply uninstall the LetterLoo app from your device
  • This will permanently remove all locally stored game progress and settings
  • There is no data stored on external servers to delete

App Permissions

LetterLoo requests minimal permissions:

  • Storage: To save game progress locally on your device

We do NOT request:

  • Camera access
  • Microphone access
  • Location access
  • Contact access
  • Internet/Network access (app works fully offline)
  • Phone state or identity

Internet Connection

LetterLoo is fully functional offline and does not require an internet connection to operate. The app does not transmit any data over the internet at any time during normal use.

Third-Party Links

LetterLoo does not contain any links to third-party websites or services. The app is self-contained and does not direct users to external content.

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

LetterLoo is designed specifically for children aged 3-6 years. We are committed to complying with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). Our app:

  • Does not collect any personal information from children
  • Does not enable children to publicly post or distribute personal information
  • Does not entice children with games or contests that require disclosure of personal information
  • Does not require registration or account creation
  • Operates entirely offline with no internet connectivity

Since no information is collected, there is no need for parental consent or notification.

Parental Control

Since LetterLoo does not collect any personal information, there is no need for parental consent or access to data. Parents have complete control over the app through their device settings and can uninstall it at any time to remove all local data.

International Users

LetterLoo can be used worldwide. All data remains on the user's device regardless of location. No cross-border data transfers occur since no data is transmitted from the app.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
